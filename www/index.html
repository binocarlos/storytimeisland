Welcome to Storytime Island

There has been a lot happening on the island this summer, the first organised kite festival was one of the highlights as well as the Littlesea carnival and Townville flower show.

The Pumpkin Festival in Townville at the weekend was a great success. Graham the Gardener won the biggest pumpkin competition with one so huge it measured about a metre across. They got it to the fair by using the crane on mechanic Mike's pickup truck!! Mrs Taylor won the pumkin pie competition and Kaylee from Little Hampton won the best carved pumpkin. Well done to all the competitors and everyone who supported the day.

There will be fireworks on Saturday the 2nd November in the square at Townville and also on the beach at Riversea, both displays start at 7pm.

Details about the Townville Christmas Fair, the Farmers Market Christmas Special and the Island Childrens Party will be on this page shortly.

CLASSES

Mrs Winter is running preserve making classes at Little Hampton Village Hall. This will be every Thursday at 1.00 pm. Learn how to use all the Autumn fruit in delicious jams and chutneys.

LOST CAT

Mrs Peters of Littlesea has lost her ginger cat and would like people in the area to keep an eye open for him. There are suspicions he got into one of the fishing boats.
