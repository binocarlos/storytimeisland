Welcome to Storytime Island

It's been a really busy year on the island. The first organised kite festival was one of the highlights as well as the Littlesea carnival and Townville flower show. Recently the Townville Pumpkin Festival was a great success as well as fireworks on the beach at Riversea. We are now on the run up to Christmas and Buddy and the rest of the team would lke to wish you compliments of the season.

Townville Christmas Fair will be held on Saturday 21st December, on the green and also in the church hall. Santa on his sleigh is due to visit and there will be rides, presents and lots of yummy food. Starting at 1pm, finishing at 5pm.

The Farmers Market Christmas Special will be on Christmas Eve so everyone can get their fresh produce for the Christmas meal. It will start at 8pm and close at 2pm. On the green in Townville as usual.

The Storytime Island Christmas Party will also be on Saturday 21st December in the Town Hall from 3 to 5pm. All children are welcome. There are special trains running from Littlesea, Little Hampton and Riversea, please contact Townville Station for details.

CLASSES

Mrs Winter's classes at Little Hampton Village Hall will now be creating things for Christmas. Mincemeat from the last of the apples, Christmas cake, Christmas Pudding then Wreaths and Garlands. Every Thursday at 1.00 pm.

LOST CAT

Mrs Peters of Littlesea is very grateful to all the people who looked for her cat, he is now safely back at home. he had gone for a ride on a fishing boat from the other side of the island! Luckily the fisherman saw the notice on this website and bought him all the way home in the boat.

SCARECROW

Fiona the Farmer sent in this photo of a scarecrow in her neighbours field, Farmer Ted. She thought if there was a competition for scarecrows this one should win it!
