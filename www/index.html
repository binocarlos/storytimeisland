Welcome to Storytime Island

There has been a lot happening on the island this summer, the first organised kite festival was one of the highlights as well as the Littlesea carnival and Townville flower show.

Now Autumn is beginning our thoughts will turn to the Pumpkin Festival and Biggest Pumpkin Competition, both to be held in Townville. Then Halloween and Fireworks.

Farmer Joe would like me to remind everyone that entries for the Pumpkin Festival and Competition have to be registered by the 15th October.

CLASSES

Mrs Winter is running preserve making classes at Little Hampton Village Hall. This will be every Thursday at 1.00 pm. Learn how to use all the Autumn fruit in delicious jams and chutneys.

Mrs Peters of Littlesea has lost her ginger cat and would like people in the area to keep an eye open for him. There are suspicions he got into one of the fishing boats.
